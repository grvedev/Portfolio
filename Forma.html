About Us

Built on care,
not corners cut

Forma Studio is a small practice based in Lisbon. We work on homes, cultural buildings, and public spaces. Every project starts the same way: we listen.

Good design is quiet. It does its job without shouting. We believe a building should feel right from the day it opens and still feel right thirty years later.
