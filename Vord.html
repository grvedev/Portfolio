The Studio

Made to last.
Designed to disappear.

VORD started with a single problem: most clothes fall apart before they get interesting. We set out to make things that age well, fit well, and stay out of your way.

Every piece is cut in our New York studio, sewn from fabrics we source directly, and checked by hand before it ships. No shortcuts, no licensing, no trend chasing.
